Trench Based Warfare

Trench Warfare is an award winning turn based strategy game about building a base and going into combat. With thousands of players, it is one of the most thrilling strategy games. You play as one of 2 soldiers initially armed with your base and only a cannon, you'll need to focus on your aim and power in order to defeat your enemy! Hit your opponent to knock them off their base or wear down their health until they fall! Earn points by making tactical shots in order to unlock a vast variety of weapons and upgrade your Base!
Cannons, Guns, Grenades. Trench Warfare has it all!
